U8's u8fonts.flx:
------------------

u8fonts.flx contains shapes with frames that map to ascii characters.

Shape 0: Normal Light Blue
Shape 1: Black (used in books and scrolls)
Shape 2: Tiny dark blue
Shape 3: Tiny white
Shape 4: Tiny black
Shape 5: Normal Orange
Shape 6: Normal Red
Shape 7: Normal Green
Shape 8: Normal Yellow
Shape 9: Small Dark Blue
Shape 10: Gold Sign (Upper & Numbers Only)
Shape 11: Tomb Stone (Upper & Numbers Only)
Shape 12: Normal Green (Numbers Only)
Shape 13: Normal Red (Numbers Only)
Shape 14: Normal Dark Blue (virtually unreadable)
Shape 15: Normal Dark Blue (virtually unreadable)
